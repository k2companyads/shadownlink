## ShadowLink Privacy Policy Last Updated: June 2026 ## Introduction ShadowLink is a privacy-focused communication platform designed to provide temporary and secure conversations. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and how users maintain control over their data. ## Information We Collect ShadowLink is designed to minimize data collection. The application may process: * Temporary room identifiers * Encrypted message payloads * Encrypted file uploads * Anonymous technical diagnostics required for service operation ShadowLink does not intentionally collect: * Message content in plain text * Contact lists * SMS content * Photos outside files explicitly shared by users * Device location * Advertising identifiers ## End-to-End Encryption Messages are encrypted on the user's device before transmission. Encryption keys are derived locally and are not transmitted to the server. The server stores encrypted content only and cannot read message contents. ## Temporary Conversations Users may configure conversations to self-destruct after a defined period. Messages marked for deletion are automatically removed according to the selected expiration policy. ## Room Access Access to private rooms requires explicit authorization from the room creator. Invitation codes alone do not guarantee access. ## SMS Invitations ShadowLink may use the device's SMS application to send invitation codes. SMS messages are transmitted through the user's mobile carrier and are not controlled by ShadowLink. Users should be aware that SMS is not an encrypted communication channel. ## Files and Voice Messages Shared files and voice recordings are encrypted before upload whenever encryption features are enabled. ## Security Features ShadowLink includes privacy-enhancing features such as: * Session expiration * Room destruction * Privacy overlays * WebRTC leak monitoring * Optional Tor-compatible usage guidance These features improve privacy but cannot guarantee complete protection against all threats. ## Donations ShadowLink may provide optional donation links through third-party payment providers. Payment processing is performed entirely by the payment provider. ShadowLink does not store payment card information or banking credentials. ## Data Retention Encrypted data is retained only for the period necessary to provide the service. Destroyed rooms and expired content are permanently removed according to system policies. ## Third-Party Services ShadowLink may rely on third-party infrastructure providers for hosting, notifications, file storage, and payment processing. Each provider operates under its own privacy policies. ## Children's Privacy ShadowLink is not intended for children under 13 years of age. ## Disclaimer While ShadowLink implements multiple privacy and security measures, no software can guarantee absolute security. Users remain responsible for protecting their devices, passwords, room codes, and personal information. ## Contact For questions regarding this Privacy Policy: Email: [[contato@shadowlink.app](mailto:contato@shadowlink.app)](mailto:contato@shadowlink.app) ## Changes This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Continued use of ShadowLink after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
